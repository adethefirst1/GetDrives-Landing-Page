About us

Moving Nigeria, Smarter

GetDrives is a Nigeria-first transportation platform built for how we actually move.

We connect everyday riders, professional drivers, and businesses through one simple system offering standard rides, shared trips, and flexible vehicle rentals. No confusion. No guesswork. Just reliable movement.

We understand the real challenges of transportation in Nigeria: unpredictable pricing, safety concerns, limited options, and wasted time. That's why GetDrives is designed to be transparent, accessible, and practical whether you're commuting to work, earning on your own schedule, or running a business that depends on mobility.

We're launching soon, and we're building this together with the people who use it. Riders, drivers, and businesses—everyone has a seat.
